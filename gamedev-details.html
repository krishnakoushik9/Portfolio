Creation Process

Divine Concept

Every sacred journey begins with a vision. My process starts with deep meditation, research into Hindu mythology, and concept art to establish the core aesthetic and gameplay mechanics. I focus on creating authentic experiences that honor the divine while providing engaging gameplay.

Sacred Prototyping

Iteration is the path to perfection. I build functional prototypes to test core mechanics, focusing on player feedback and spiritual feel before investing in high-fidelity assets. This approach allows me to align with the divine vision when the current path isn't working.

Temple Building

With the divine blueprint established, I move into full production, creating detailed temple environments, deity models, sacred animations, and implementing advanced systems like spiritual AI behavior trees, quest logic, and optimization for various hardware configurations.
